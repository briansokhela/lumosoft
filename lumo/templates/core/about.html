About Lumo Software Solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Lumo Software Solutions began as a small team of passionate developers with a vision to transform businesses through technology.

Over the years, we've grown into a full-service software development company, serving clients across various industries including healthcare, finance, education, and retail.

Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation to emerging technologies, and an unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional value to our clients.
